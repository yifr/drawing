Description instructions


Your descriptions will be looked at by the experimenters. As long as we determine that your descriptions were any reasonable, understandable attempt to describe the original image, you will receive a bonus for that description. You will only fail to receive this bonus if you do not attempt to describe the particular image that you saw.

Other instructions to keep in mind:

  • Your descriptions will be looked at in order with all of the original images that you see in the experiment. It's OK to reference what you said before in an earlier description.
  • It is OK to be creative! You can use any description that another English speaking person would understand if they had gone through the experiment with you, and were looking at the same picture.
  • Some of the pictures you see may be quite complicated and difficult to describe perfectly. It’s OK if you don’t describe every last detail, however, we do ask that you do your best to describe the picture you see.
